          Data Sources#

          Public data resources for understanding Winnipeg’s urban development, planning, transportation, and housing landscape.

          City of Winnipeg#

          • Winnipeg Open Data Portal — The city’s open data catalogue, including cycling network maps, property data, permits, and more
          • City of Winnipeg Zoning — Zoning by-law, maps, and amendment applications
          • Development Applications — Active development permits and rezoning applications
          • Council Meeting Agendas & Minutes — Full text of Council and Committee agenda packages
          • OurWinnipeg 2045 — Development plan and supporting direction strategies
          • 2024 Infrastructure Plan — Capital investment priorities tied to planning

          Property and Assessment#

          • Winnipeg Assessment & Taxation — Property assessment data and tax information
          • Winnipeg Property Online (Assessment Roll) — Search property assessments by address or roll number

          Transportation#

          • Winnipeg Transit — Routes & Schedules — Current transit routes, schedules, and service alerts
          • Winnipeg Transit Master Plan — The long-range plan for Winnipeg Transit through 2045
          • Cycling Network Map (Open Data) — Current cycling infrastructure across Winnipeg
          • Walk Score — Winnipeg — Walkability and transit scores by address

          Provincial and Federal#

          • Statistics Canada — Winnipeg Census Profile — Population, housing, and income data from the 2021 Census
          • Canada Mortgage and Housing Corporation (CMHC) — National housing market data and rental market reports
          • Manitoba Land Initiative (MLI) — Provincial mapping and land registry data
          • Manitoba Government — Historic Resources Branch — Heritage building designations and historical resources

          Community and Advocacy#

          • Bike Winnipeg — Cycling advocacy and infrastructure priorities
          • YIMBY Winnipeg — Pro-housing advocacy and zoning reform analysis
          • Engage Winnipeg — Active city consultations and public engagement
