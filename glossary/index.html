          Glossary

          Glossary#

          Plain-language definitions for urban planning terms that come up in Winnipeg City Council meetings, Community Committee hearings, and community discussions.

          The glossary is organized into four categories:

          • Housing & Zoning — Secondary suites, variances, missing middle, heritage overlays
          • Transportation — BRT, complete streets, active transportation, mode share
          • Land Use — OurWinnipeg, Complete Communities, infill, greenfield
          • Funding & Policy — TIF, Tax Increment Financing, Housing Accelerator Fund, development levies
