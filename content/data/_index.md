# Data Sources

Public data resources for understanding Winnipeg's urban development, planning, transportation, and housing landscape.

## City of Winnipeg

- **[Winnipeg Open Data Portal](https://data.winnipeg.ca/)** — The city's open data catalogue, including cycling network maps, property data, permits, and more
- **[City of Winnipeg Zoning](https://www.winnipeg.ca/building-development/permits-development/zoning)** — Zoning by-law, maps, and amendment applications
- **[Development Applications](https://www.winnipeg.ca/building-development/permits-development/development-applications)** — Active development permits and rezoning applications
- **[Council Meeting Agendas & Minutes](https://clkapps.winnipeg.ca/)** — Full text of Council and Committee agenda packages
- **[OurWinnipeg 2045](https://www.winnipeg.ca/building-development/city-planning-design/ourwinnipeg)** — Development plan and supporting direction strategies
- **[2024 Infrastructure Plan](https://www.winnipeg.ca/city-governance/documents-reports/2024-infrastructure-plan)** — Capital investment priorities tied to planning

## Property and Assessment

- **[Winnipeg Assessment & Taxation](https://www.winnipeg.ca/assessment/)** — Property assessment data and tax information
- **[Winnipeg Property Online (Assessment Roll)](https://assessment.winnipeg.ca/)** — Search property assessments by address or roll number

## Transportation

- **[Winnipeg Transit — Routes & Schedules](https://winnipegtransit.com/)** — Current transit routes, schedules, and service alerts
- **[Winnipeg Transit Master Plan](https://info.winnipegtransit.com/en/major-projects/transit-master-plan/)** — The long-range plan for Winnipeg Transit through 2045
- **[Cycling Network Map (Open Data)](https://data.winnipeg.ca/Transportation-Planning-Traffic-Management/Cycling-Network-Map/e9ms-78q6)** — Current cycling infrastructure across Winnipeg
- **[Walk Score — Winnipeg](https://www.walkscore.com/score/winnipeg)** — Walkability and transit scores by address

## Provincial and Federal

- **[Statistics Canada — Winnipeg Census Profile](https://www12.statcan.gc.ca/census-recensement/2021/dp-pd/prof/index.cfm?Lang=E)** — Population, housing, and income data from the 2021 Census
- **[Canada Mortgage and Housing Corporation (CMHC)](https://www.cmhc-schl.gc.ca/)** — National housing market data and rental market reports
- **[Manitoba Land Initiative (MLI)](https://www.gov.mb.ca/ia/programs/mli2.html)** — Provincial mapping and land registry data
- **[Manitoba Government — Historic Resources Branch](https://www.gov.mb.ca/chc/hrb/index.html)** — Heritage building designations and historical resources

## Community and Advocacy

- **[Bike Winnipeg](https://www.bikewinnipeg.ca/)** — Cycling advocacy and infrastructure priorities
- **[YIMBY Winnipeg](https://www.yesinwpg.com/)** — Pro-housing advocacy and zoning reform analysis
- **[Engage Winnipeg](https://engage.winnipeg.ca/)** — Active city consultations and public engagement
