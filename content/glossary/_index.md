# Glossary

Plain-language definitions for urban planning terms that come up in Winnipeg City Council meetings, Community Committee hearings, and community discussions.

The glossary is organized into four categories:

- **[Housing & Zoning]({{< relref "housing-zoning" >}})** — Secondary suites, variances, missing middle, heritage overlays
- **[Transportation]({{< relref "transportation" >}})** — BRT, complete streets, active transportation, mode share
- **[Land Use]({{< relref "land-use" >}})** — OurWinnipeg, Complete Communities, infill, greenfield
- **[Funding & Policy]({{< relref "funding-policy" >}})** — TIF, Tax Increment Financing, Housing Accelerator Fund, development levies
